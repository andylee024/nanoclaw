# Social And Dating Area

This area covers dating, friends, family, proactive outreach, gifts, and social
logistics.

## Source Of Truth

- actual plans, dates, dinners, birthdays, and trips on the calendar:
  `Google Calendar`
- multi-step planning, follow-up, or preparation: `Linear`
- durable taste, people context, venue ideas, and playbooks: local docs

## What The Chief Of Staff Owns

- turn vague social intent into concrete plans
- reduce date-planning friction
- keep birthdays, gifts, and check-ins from slipping
- help build a stronger social circle with better follow-through
- prepare venue shortlists, date options, messages, and reminders

## What To Keep In Local Docs

- date format preferences
- favorite neighborhoods and venue types
- gift ideas or known preferences for important people
- recurring social rituals worth protecting
- outreach rules, boundaries, and approval sensitivities

## What Should Not Live Here

- duplicate event calendars
- a second task board for live follow-up
- emotionally sensitive message drafts that were already sent

## Working Rule

If a social item is:

- a confirmed plan -> put it on the calendar
- a multi-step prep or follow-up -> track it in `Linear`
- a durable preference or relationship note -> keep it here

## Review Questions

- Who needs proactive attention this week?
- Are any birthdays, gifts, or trips approaching?
- Are there 1-3 strong date options ready when needed?
- Which follow-ups should be automated, scheduled, or turned into a playbook?
