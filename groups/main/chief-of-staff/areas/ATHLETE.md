# Athlete Area

This area covers workout support, schedule protection, recovery logistics, and
athletic life planning.

## Source Of Truth

- workout planning and logging: dedicated training runtime
  (`telegram_clawfitness`, `whatsapp_clawfitness`, or equivalent)
- training data and progression: mounted `Train` project
- actual scheduled classes, runs, games, and sessions: `Google Calendar`

Do not duplicate detailed workout logs in Markdown.

## What Main Chief Of Staff Owns

- protect training blocks from avoidable schedule collisions
- surface conflicts between work, dating, travel, and training
- help with gear, memberships, classes, and programming logistics
- track injuries, recovery constraints, and environment constraints at a high
  level
- prepare weekly summaries and decisions, not raw set-by-set logs

## What Dedicated Training Runtime Owns

- today's plan
- workout logging
- progression history
- exercise-specific queries
- training-specific clarification

## Useful Durable Context To Keep Here

- current training priorities
- current constraints or injuries
- preferred gyms, courts, classes, studios, and coaches
- gear or replacement needs
- recurring events that affect training consistency

## Weekly Review Questions

- What training sessions matter most this week?
- What calendar conflicts threaten them?
- What recovery bottlenecks need action?
- Are there purchases, bookings, or class decisions to make?
- Does anything from training need to be turned into a chief-of-staff task?
